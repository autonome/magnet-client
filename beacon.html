Fight for the Open Web! Using Minecraft for Internet Activism

The participants will work in teams of 2-3 to create elements of what will eventually become an interactive city comprised of everybody's work.

Each team (or individual, depending on size) will build their creation in a designated plot of Minecraft land in the "city". They will then go through their creation with the session leader and explain how they want it to be interactive or animated. They can then watch < 5 minutes of their imagination turning into Java code and then see it come to life in the game.

At the end there will be a 15-20 minute presentation period where all participants present their creation in front of the others and show off its interactivity.
